Adspirer is an <Tooltip tip="Model Context Protocol — an open standard for connecting AI assistants to external tools">MCP</Tooltip> server that connects AI assistants to advertising platforms. Ask ChatGPT, Claude, or any MCP-compatible client to manage your Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and TikTok Ads campaigns — no dashboards, no learning curve.
