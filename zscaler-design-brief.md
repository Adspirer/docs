## Brand & Visual Direction

### Dual Branding
- **Top of page 1:** Adspirer logo (left) + Zscaler logo (right), connected by a subtle line or "Ã—" symbol
- Adspirer logo files: `logo/dark.png` and `logo/light.png` in the docs repo
- Zscaler brand: Use their official logo (blue wordmark). Reference: [zscaler.com/press/brand-assets](https://www.zscaler.com/press/brand-assets)
- Maintain equal visual weight â€” this is a partnership document

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Adspirer blue (#2563EB)
- **Secondary:** Zscaler blue (#0090D1)
- **Accent:** Warm gold/amber for highlights, CTAs, and roadmap milestones
- **Neutrals:** Dark charcoal (#0F172A) for text, light grays for backgrounds, white for breathing room

### Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
- Headlines: Bold, large, confident
- Body: Regular weight, generous line spacing
- Data callouts: Extra-bold numbers with light supporting text

### Visual Style
- Minimal, editorial, lots of whitespace
- Subtle gradients (not flat, not skeuomorphic)
- Icon-driven sections
- Data visualizations: clean and elegant

## Key Design Principles

1. **Lead with the mission, not the product.** "AI advertising agents for every business" is bigger than a feature list. Make it feel aspirational.
2. **This is NOT a sales deck.** It's a strategic partnership document. Consulting firm presenting a roadmap, not a vendor pitching.
3. **The staircase is the signature visual.** The ascending 4-phase roadmap on page 3 should be the most memorable graphic in the PDF.
4. **Performance Analysis is the hero capability.** Column 1 on page 2 should be visually dominant.
5. **Security is brief and confident.** One strip, four signals, done. Zscaler will do their own diligence.
6. **White space is your friend.** Three pages is tight. Every element earns its space.
7. **The wireframe letters (Aâ€“N) are your map.** Reference them in design reviews.
